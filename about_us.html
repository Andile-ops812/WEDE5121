About Us

Our History

Mercedes-Benz is one of the world's most well-known luxury automobile manufacturers. The company's history dates to the invention of the first petrol-powered automobile by Karl Benz in 1886. Benz created the three-wheeled Benz Patent-Motorwagen, which is widely considered the first practical automobile.

In 1901, the first car branded "Mercedes" was introduced after an investor suggested naming the vehicle after his daughter, Mercedes. The name quickly became associated with innovative and high-performance vehicles.
